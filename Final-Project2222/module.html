What is a BAR CHART?

A bar chart is a graphical representation used to display and compare categorical data through rectangular bars, where the length or height of each bar is proportional to the frequency or value of the corresponding category.

Categorical data is a collection of information that is divided into groups but cannot be ordered or measured
